Tetris: The Grand Master

Posted on November 5, 2025, updated semi-regularly

Today, I finally have a working Tetris: The Grand Master setup (abbreviated as TGM) on a MAME emulator.

If you're unfamiliar with what any of what I just said is, don't worry. All you need to know is that It's Tetris, But Hard.

I haven't booted the game up yet. I'm excited. I'm worried that I won't like the 8bitdo's SN30 Pro Plus controller's d-pad, and that I may have to switch to using my Steam Deck (I like clicky d-pads.), or find an alternative (I was originally gonna buy the arcade archives on the switch, but on second thought, the Switch Lite's d-pad has far too many accidental inputs to genuinely consider using. Also, I heard it has a tiny bit of extra input lag).

I'm ready. I enjoy a good Tetris. This is exciting and terrifying. Will I be up for the challenge? I hope so.
